About Hotel Template

A story woven into every stay.

Our Journey

Hotel Template began as a dream to craft spaces where travelers don’t just rest—they rediscover. From our first brick to today, we’ve built a haven that blends curiosity with comfort, rooted in a love for the unexpected.

Our History

Founded in 2010, we started with a single room—a prototype of what a stay could be. Over the years, we’ve grown, but our core remains: every guest is part of our story.

Our Mission

To inspire wanderers with spaces that feel like home, yet spark adventure. We’re here to turn moments into memories.
